Polymaze 3D

Project information

  • Category: Game Development
  • Client: Polytechnic University of the Philippines Paranaque Campus
  • Project date: March 2021
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/embed/n3_Vdb40pKU

    • Polymaze 3D is an engaging game that transports players to a diverse and exciting virtual world. Each landscape in this realm presents its own unique challenges and delightful surprises, ensuring that no two moments in the game are the same. Players begin their journey with three lives, which they must use wisely to overcome the obstacles in their path. A key aspect of this adventure is the strategic collection of coins. By skillfully amassing these valuable tokens, players can earn additional lives, which extend their lifeline and allow them to continue their quest. In Polymaze 3D, collecting coins is not just a means to progress, but also a thrilling aspect of the adventure itself, enhancing the overall gaming experience.
