The RFID Monitoring System for the attendance of students at PUP Parañaque Campus is a technologically advanced and automated solution designed to efficiently and accurately track the presence and attendance of students within the campus premises. Leveraging Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) technology, this system assigns unique RFID cards to each student, which are then scanned upon entry to designated areas or classrooms. This process ensures real-time and secure attendance recording, minimizes the risk of attendance fraud, and provides administrators with comprehensive data for attendance management and reporting. By implementing this system, PUP Parañaque Campus enhances operational efficiency, reinforces attendance integrity, and contributes to an improved educational environment.
